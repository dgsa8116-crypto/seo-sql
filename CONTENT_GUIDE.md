# Content Guide

## Tone

Use polished Traditional Chinese. Keep the tone educational, direct, and calm. Avoid hype, urgency, fake authority, fake reviews, and income promises.

## Required Framing

Every guide page should reinforce:

- The content is informational.
- Results are uncertain.
- No method guarantees profit.
- Adults must follow local law.
- Entertainment spending should be limited and voluntary.

## Page Types

- Rules guide: explain card values, banker/player/tie, natural hands, and third-card rules.
- Betting flow guide: explain process and risk without recommending bets.
- Beginner guide: explain learning order and common mistakes.
- Terminology guide: define terms without implying prediction.
- Responsible entertainment page: include age, regional, and risk notices.
- FAQ: answer real questions and avoid fabricated claims.

## Disallowed Copy

Do not claim:

- Guaranteed winning
- Guaranteed profit
- Risk-free betting
- Certain income
- Secret winning methods
- Betting loopholes
- Platform exploits
- Underage access
- Illegal bypass methods
